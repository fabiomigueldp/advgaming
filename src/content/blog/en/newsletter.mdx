## Mailchimp powered newsletters

Adventury Network creates multiple newsletter forms with support for lists and tags, using Astro and StarFunnel following industry best practices.

You can configure your newsletters in the CMS under **Settings > Contact, Social & Forms.** Set up global newsletter settings and create lists that can be opened via anchor link.

Set your mailchimp credentials in your .env and in your netlify/vercel deploy settings

```
MAILCHIMP_API_KEY=XXX
MAILCHIMP_SERVER_PREFIX=us21	
MAILCHIMP_LIST_ID=XXX
```
